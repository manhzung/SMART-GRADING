\begin{center}
    \Large{\textbf{ABSTRACT}}\\
\end{center}
\vspace{1cm}

In the context of modern education, digitizing and automating assessment processes has become an inevitable trend, especially as multiple-choice testing is widely adopted across all educational levels. However, traditional manual multiple-choice grading still presents significant drawbacks: time-consuming procedures, error-prone due to human factors, and requiring costly specialized equipment. Although several multiple-choice grading applications exist in the market, most only provide basic score reporting without offering in-depth analytical insights for teachers.

This thesis focuses on researching and building the SMART GRADING mobile application, which enables teachers to use smartphone cameras to automatically scan and grade multiple-choice answer sheets. The system integrates Optical Character Recognition (OCR) techniques to extract answers from photographed answer sheets, combined with advanced image processing algorithms such as document corner detection, perspective transformation, and answer bubble segmentation. Additionally, the system incorporates Generative AI to analyze exam results, automatically generate score distribution summaries, identify frequently missed questions, and provide detailed feedback, offering teachers comprehensive insights to adjust their teaching methods promptly.

Experimental results demonstrate that the application operates stably on real devices, achieves high accuracy in answer recognition, and the AI integration delivers superior analytical value compared to conventional multiple-choice grading solutions available on the market.
